FTC - Artemis

Founded in 2009 with limited resources and no experience whatsoever, the Prototypes tackled the challenge and took their robot to the Sacramento Regionals. Against all odds, the Prototypes were given the “Rookie Inspiration Award” and attained the highest rookie seed. The Prototypes experienced impediments throughout the years through rough transitions in leadership and lost knowledge. In 2013, the Prototypes were bestowed with the “Judge’s Award” due to their creative usage of materials under a limited budget. Now, the Prototypes continue to foster excellence by exposing their campus to the ingenuity of FIRST. In 2018, the Prototypes won the “Engineering Inspiration Award” at the San Francisco Valley Regional alongside a ticket to Worlds. Hungry for more, they persevered through the 7th seed to the 2nd seed at the offseason CalGames tournament and won.
